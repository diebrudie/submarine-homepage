FAQs

Here are a few additional resources to help you get started.
What is pinata?
Pinata is a media distribution platform that powers a variety of marketplaces, metaverses and NFT projects, featuring a suite of tools to help creators upload, manage and distribute content quickly and securely. Submarine is built using Pinata’s Submarine API.
How does Submarining work?
Normal uploads to Pinata are public, but any file you Submarine is stored privately on our servers. Submarining allows you to choose who can access those files, and how they do it.
When should I think about using Submarine?
If you have an existing social media, blog or newsletter audience that you want to offer exclusive content to, Submarine is for you. If you’re a new creator or you’re not sure how Submarining could work for you, contact our community team and we can chat through your use case.
How can I monetize my content with Submarine?
One of the most common ways creators monetize with Submarine is through NFTs. You can decide how much to charge for your NFT, and then give access to exclusive content only to holders of your NFT. Again, feel free to contact us if you need help figuring out the best approach.
Are there any additional use cases I can read through?
Definitely. Check out How To Build Your Own NFT-Gated No-Code Version Of Udemy and How to Enable Members-Only File Access with Pinata and Unlock Membership NFTs.
I’m already using Pinata. Do I need to sign up for Submarine separately?
Nope! You can use your existing Pinata account to access Submarine directly if you are on a paid plan.
How long are Submarine links valid for?
Submarine links are valid for as long as you have your account with Pinata!
